Unfledged Idealist

Honestly, I have loved painting and game-level designing since I was young, however, my parents' recommendation and the difficult employment situation have pushed me to a completely different path, Computer Science. To my surprise, in this seemingly different field, I unexpectedly encountered the perfect combination of Computer Science and visual communication, Computer Graphics. Computer Graphics could output works comparable to that of a professional painter in a few minutes with the prompt of several precise instructions, which greatly reduces onerous work. As an important branch of computer science technology, it has received great attention from many practitioners, including me, in recent years. I am eager to optimize these unique computer technologies to produce more creative applications and games.
